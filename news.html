TWS competes in 2015 "Park Tank"

The International Parking Institute (IPI) selected TWS as one of six finalists to present at "Park Tank" during the IPI 2015 conference in Las Vegas. On June 29th Dr. Santanu Dutta presented TWS's BLE based garage parking concept to a panel of four judges. TWS also particpated with 35 other companies in the poster session, highlighting TWS's innovative garage and street parking payment model, as well as demoing the Android application.
