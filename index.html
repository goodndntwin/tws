Mission

Create patent protected, licensable, wireless technology concepts and demonstrations, applicable to cashless parking payment. We bring several decades of seminal R&D experience in the latest wireless technologies.


We offer

  • Six granted patents covering both on-street and gated-garage parking payment systems
  • Detailed descriptive material, including videos, about our system concepts
  • Professional consulting services to help implement our concepts
  • All patents are available for purchase or license

Distinguishing Features of our Methods & Systems

  • Focus on user experience (best-in-class, as measured by user actions required for a payment transaction)
  • Supported by present smartphones – both iOS and Android
  • Support for Enforcement Officer productivity enhancement
  • Better suited for integration into vehicle’s electronic system than other parking payment systems
  • Recognized by International Parking and Mobility Institute (IPMI)
